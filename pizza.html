Pizza

Preparation Time: 20 mins

Difficulty: Moderate

Ingredients

  • flour
  • Yeast and water
  • Oil and seasoning
  • sauce
  • Cheese
  • Herbs

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, yeast, water, and salt. Knead well and let it rest.
  2. Roll the dough into a flat base and place it on a baking tray.
  3. Add pizza sauce, cheese, and your favorite toppings.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C for 15–20 minutes.
