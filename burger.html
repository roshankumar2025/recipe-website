Burger

Preparation Time: 20 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Ground chuck
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese
  • Sauce

Instructions

  1. Season the ground meat and shape into patties.
  2. Cook the patties on a pan or grill.
  3. Toast buns and assemble with cheese and sauce.
