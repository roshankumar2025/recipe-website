Momos

Preparation Time: 400 mins

Difficulty: Moderate

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Table salt and water
  • Ground meat or finely chopped veg
  • Onion and ginger-garlic paste
  • Soy sauce and pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, salt, and water to form a stiff dough
  2. Mix all filling ingredients
  3. Roll dough into circles
  4. Pinch and fold the edges together to seal the dumpling
  5. Grease a steamer and cook for 10 mins
